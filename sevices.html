Our Services

PetUniverse Connect offers a comprehensive suite of services designed to meet every pet owner's needs. Our platform facilitates connections between pet lovers and trusted professionals.

Easily book appointments with certified veterinarians for check-ups, emergencies, and specialized treatments.

Professional Grooming

Find and schedule grooming services, from simple baths to full styling and pet spa experiences.

Pet Sitting & Boarding

Connect with trusted pet sitters and boarding facilities for when you're away.

Pet Supplies & Nutrition

Access recommendations and resources for high-quality pet food, toys, and accessories.

Dog Walking & Training

Find experienced dog walkers and certified trainers to keep your pet happy and well-behaved.

Community Events & Forums

Join local pet events, workshops, and engage in our forums for advice and discussions.
